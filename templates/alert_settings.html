How alerts work

After each Govee sensor sync (every 10 minutes), the app compares the reading against the fruiting temperature and humidity ranges for every active batch in that chamber. If a reading is out of range, a streak counter increments. At 3 consecutive violations (~30 minutes) you receive one SMS.

The alert is not repeated until the reading returns to range and goes out again — so a chamber that stays cold won't flood your phone. When the reading recovers, the streak and alert flag reset automatically.

Parameters monitored: Temperature (°F), Humidity (% RH), and CO₂ (ppm) when available. Thresholds are the species' full acceptable fruiting range from the species configuration, not the batch target midpoint.
